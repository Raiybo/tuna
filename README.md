# 🐟 Tuna — live bluefin map (Lebanon)

Public viewer for the daily tuna day-sheet off **Marina Dbayeh**. Open it on your phone:

**→ https://raiybo.github.io/tuna/**

- Live sea conditions (Open-Meteo) computed in your browser — always current.
- Switch to a **Satellite** layer (top-right) for the latest free NASA imagery; **tap any spot for its GPS** + a 10 m Sentinel-2 image.
- 💥 markers are logged frenzy / bird sightings.

> Satellites can’t see a live surface bust (too small & brief) — use them to find blooms & colour edges, then confirm with birds on the water. Spots are search zones, not guaranteed marks; bluefin are regulated — check seasons, quotas & permits.

The full engine (scoring, CLI, daily automation) lives in a separate private repo; this repo is just the public map.
